What's Chat Alerts?

Chat Alerts is for small streamers to help interact with their chat. Streamers that need to constantly look at chat to check for new messages can get distracted from their stream content which drops the quality of the stream.

It fits the needs for those small streamers by sending out audio notifications to hint the streamer for new chat messages.
