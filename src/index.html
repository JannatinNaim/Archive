What is Chat Alerts?

Chat Alerts notifies you with audio cues for messages in your Twitch chat. You can get notified for all messages, messages from specific users or even custom regex matches.

Streamers that constantly need to look at chat loose focus from making the actual content be of utmost quality. Chat Alerts saves them in this regard by letting them know when they should be looking at chat.

That's why Chat Alerts has a motto of, Focus on what's important.
